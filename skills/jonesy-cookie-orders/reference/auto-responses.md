# Auto-Response Templates

## Quick Reference

Use these templates verbatim or adapt as needed. Every response should sound like Thurman texting — warm, direct, no corporate language.

---

## First Response (New Inquiry)

### Standard Event Inquiry
```
Hey [NAME]! Thanks for reaching out about your [EVENT TYPE] — appreciate you finding us!

Got a few quick questions back to you so I can put together an accurate quote:

- What date and about what time?
- How many people are you expecting?
- Any flavors in mind, or should I guide you?
- Where's the venue — in DFW or nearby?

I'll get you a number as soon as I have those. Excited to be part of your [EVENT TYPE]!
```

### Quick Question (Non-Event)
```
Hey [NAME]! Good question — [ANSWER HERE]. 

Let me know if you need anything else!
```

### After Hours
```
Hey [NAME]! I appreciate you reaching out. I'm away from the oven right now but will get back to you first thing tomorrow morning.

Talk soon!
```

## Complaint Response (Escalate First)

Do NOT respond publicly to complaints. Alert Thurman immediately:

```
⚠️ COMPLAINT — [PLATFORM]
From: [NAME]
Event: [DATE]
Stars: [X]

"[REVIEW TEXT]"

Action: Flagged for Thurman's immediate attention.
```

Then DM Thurman directly with the full context.

---

## Rules

1. **Always respond as Thurman** — not a CSR, not a brand
2. **Short paragraphs** — one idea per line
3. **No emoji abuse** — 1-3 max, and only if they fit naturally
4. **No "we're excited to announce"** language
5. **End with a question or CTA** — give them somewhere to go next
6. **Match their energy** — formal inquiry gets slightly more formal response, casual gets casual
