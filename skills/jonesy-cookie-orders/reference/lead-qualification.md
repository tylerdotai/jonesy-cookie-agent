# Lead Qualification Reference

## Purpose

Every inquiry needs to be qualified before a quote is sent. A qualified lead has enough information to make a decision and has a real event with a real date.

---

## Questions to Ask

### Discovery Script (Text/Email)

```
Hey [NAME]! Thanks for reaching out — excited to hear about your event!

A few quick questions so I can put together an accurate quote:

1. What kind of event are you planning?
   (wedding / corporate / birthday / other)
2. What date? And what's the general time you need the cookies delivered?
3. About how many people are you expecting?
4. Any flavor preferences? (our classics vs premium tiers)
5. Where's the venue — in DFW or outside the area?
6. How did you find us? (Google / Facebook / referral / other)
```

### What to Capture

| Field | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| Event type | Determines scope, packaging, setup needs |
| Event date | Determines lead time, availability |
| Headcount | Determines dozen count and pricing tier |
| Location | Determines delivery fee, travel feasibility |
| Flavors | Determines kitchen prep and special orders |
| Budget | Helpful for steering toward right tier |
| Source | Knows what's working for acquisition |

---

## Qualification Status

### QUALIFIED — Ready for Quote
- All 6 discovery questions answered
- Event date is confirmed or clearly imminent
- Headcount is specific or clearly estimated
- Location is DFW or explicitly agreed to delivery outside

### NEEDS MORE INFO — Not Ready
- Missing event date
- "Sometime in May" without a target date
- Headcount unknown
- No response to follow-up in 48h

### NOT A FIT — Handle Gracefully
- Event outside DFW with no delivery arrangement
- Date that has already passed
- Request for services Jonesy's doesn't offer (e.g., full catering meals)
- Budget incompatible even with entry tier

---

## Response When Not Qualified

```
Hey [NAME] — thanks for reaching out!

I'd love to help. A couple quick things:

To put together a quote, I just need to know:
- Roughly when your event is
- About how many people you're expecting
- What flavors you're thinking

Once I have those, I'll get you a solid number right away. Sound good?
```

---

## Qualifying Budget Questions

If budget comes up organically, be honest:

```
Hey [NAME] — our cookies start at $[PRICE] for a dozen of our Gourmet Classics, and go up from there depending on the flavors and any custom work.

Happy to work within a budget — what are you working with?
```

Don't push budget if they don't offer it. Let them ask.
