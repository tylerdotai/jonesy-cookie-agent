# Response Templates Reference

## By Rating Level

---

## 5-Stars — Respond Within 24h

**Response:**
```
[NAME] — you're going to make me blush. Seriously, this is why we do this.

Hope to see you at the next event! If you ever need cookies or know someone who does, we're always here. 🍪

— Thurman
Jonesy's Cookie Company
```

**When to use:**
- Genuine 5-star reviews
- Specific praise mentioned (flavor, service, product)
- First-time or repeat customer

---

## 4-Stars — Thank + Ask

**Response:**
```
Hey [NAME] — really appreciate the kind words! Almost perfect is still pretty great.

Anything you wish was different? Always trying to improve. And if there was something that almost kept you from 5 stars, I'd love to know. We genuinely listen. 🍪

— Thurman
```

**When to use:**
- "Good but not great" reviews
- Reviews that mention something positive and something negative
- Reviews that seem like they wanted to give 5 but held back

---

## 3-Stars or Below — Escalate Immediately

**Do NOT respond publicly.** Flag Thurman:

```
⚠️ REVIEW ALERT — [PLATFORM]
[NAME] left [X] stars.
Event: [DATE]
Review text: "[EXCERPT]"

Flag reason: [BELOW 3 STARS / COMPLAINT / PRESSING ISSUE]

Action: Waiting for Thurman's approval before responding.
```

**Then text Thurman directly with full context.**

---

## Review Examples to Learn From

**Good 5-star response style:**
> "That's the kind of note that makes the early mornings worth it. We're just a local baker who takes this seriously — and it's people like you that make it worth it. See you at the next event!"

**What to avoid:**
> "Thank you so much for your 5-star review! We are thrilled to hear you enjoyed your experience with Jonesy's Cookie Company! Please visit us again soon!"

That sounds like a chain. Don't be a chain.
