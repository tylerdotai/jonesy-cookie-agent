# Referral Nurturing Reference

## When to Spot a Referral Opportunity

After a confirmed event, evaluate whether the client is referral-ready:

---

## Referral Indicators

### High Probability (Flag Immediately)
- Client mentioned they want to "send friends" or "share this with coworkers"
- Wedding planner or event coordinator booked on behalf of a client
- Corporate HR or office manager ordered for a team event
- High headcount event (20+ dozen) with enthusiastic response
- Client is a local business owner or community influencer
- Event was flawless and client expressed strong gratitude

### Medium Probability (Include in Weekly Digest)
- Happy customer, standard headcount
- Client is active on social media (seems like someone who'd share)
- Event was a personal celebration (birthday, graduation)
- Client specifically asked about flavors or pricing (engaged buyer)

---

## How to Nurture a Referral

### Immediately After Event (Same Day)
Send thank-you + subtle referral prompt:

```
[NAME] — it was a blast being part of your [EVENT]! Hope those cookies hit the spot.

If you ever need cookies again or know someone who does, send them our way. And if you're happy with how things went, a Google or Facebook review helps small businesses like ours more than you'd believe!

🍪 Thanks again!
— Thurman
(817) 205-1238
```

### 1 Week After Event (If No Referral Activity)
```
Hey [NAME] — just wanted to say thanks again for having us at your [EVENT]. We really enjoyed it!

If you know anyone else planning something sweet this season, we'd love the introduction. And if you're happy with how things went, a review on Google or Facebook really does help us grow.

Thanks so much!
— Thurman
```

### Referral Intro Message (For Thurman to Send)
When a client says "I'll send people your way":

```
Hey [NAME] — thanks so much for that! I'd love to meet anyone you're comfortable introducing.

Here's our info:
📞 (817) 205-1238
🌐 jonesyscookies.com
📍 Corinth, TX | DFW

Appreciate you thinking of us!
```

## Referral Tracking Log

Track referrals in `research/referrals/[year].md`:

```
| DATE       | REFERRED BY    | REFERRED TO    | EVENT        | STATUS   |
|------------|---------------|---------------|--------------|----------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | [NAME]        | [NAME/CO]     | [EVENT TYPE] | [STATUS] |
```

**STATUS options:**
- `introduced` — Client gave a name
- `contacted` — Referred person reached out
- `booked` — Referred person confirmed a booking
- `paid` — Revenue from referral received
