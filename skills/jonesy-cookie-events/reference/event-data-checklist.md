## Venue-Specific Notes

### Wedding
- Ask about setup time — venues often need vendors in 1-2h before doors
- Note headcount vs. cookie count — don't let them order too few
- Ask about cake cutting — are the cookies a replacement or supplement?
- Confirm access/parking for load-in

### Corporate
- Ask about dietary restrictions — HR may know of allergies
- Note if they want branded packaging (extra cost, extra lead time)
- Ask if there's a specific contact person on-site day-of
- Ask about load-in access / elevator / stairs

### Birthday Party
- Ask about age group — kids vs. adults changes portion sizing
- Note if it's at a venue or home
- Birthday person's favorite flavor — opportunity for a special touch

### Farmers Market
- Set start/end hours clearly
- Note booth location and whether Jonesy's provides a table
- Confirm power access
- Note if market has any specific rules about samples
