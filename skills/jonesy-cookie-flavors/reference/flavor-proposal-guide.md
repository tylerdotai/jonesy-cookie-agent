# Flavor Proposal Guide

## How to Write a Flavor Proposal

Use this guide when proposing a new flavor to Thurman or drafting launch copy for an upcoming seasonal drop.

---

## The Proposal Framework

Every flavor proposal needs:

### 1. Name
The hook. Make it memorable, specific, and appetizing.

**Good:** "Churro Delight"
**Bad:** "Cinnamon Sugar Cookie"
**Bad:** "Cinnamon Snickerdoodle variant"

### 2. Description
2-3 sentences. Describe the taste, texture, and inspiration in plain language.

```
A thick, chewy cookie loaded with cinnamon sugar and vanilla cream cheese frosting. Inspired by the churros at the Fort Worth Stockyards — the version you'd actually eat instead of taking a photo of.
```

### 3. Tier
Is this a **Gourmet Classic** (timeless, simple) or a **Premium Tier** (loaded, decadent)?

### 4. Tagline
One punchy line for social. The Instagram caption opener.

```
"The one that made me eat my entire R&D batch."
```

### 5. Image Prompt
See `./image-prompts.md` in `jonesy-cookie-social/` for the base template.

### 6. Why It Works
Why this flavor, this season, this audience?

```
- Fits the fall/winter comfort food mood
- Different from Crumbl's typical offerings
- Can be made in large batches for events
- Appeals to both traditional and adventurous cookie eaters
```

### 7. Proposed Launch Date
When should this go live?

### 8. Campaign Hook
How do we launch it? (Limited run, Event Tie-In, Flavor Showdown, Insider Drop)

## Example Flavor Proposal

```
🍪 PROPOSED FLAVOR: Honey Walnut

Description:
A butter-rich cookie loaded with crunchy walnuts and swirled with local honey. Not too sweet, deeply satisfying — like the cookies your grandmother made but better. A nod to the Texas honey producers in the area.

Price tier: Gourmet Classic

Tagline:
"When you eat one, you immediately want another."

Image prompt:
Thick honey walnut cookie, golden brown, walnuts visible in cross-section, on natural slate background, warm kitchen lighting, top-down angle. No text overlay.

Why it works:
- Texas honey is a regional point of pride
- Not a flashy flavor — appeals to adults and kids
- Walnuts are affordable and available year-round
- Complements the existing menu without overlapping

Proposed launch: March 1, 2026
Campaign hook: Limited Run — "Only here through spring"
```

## How to Evaluate a Flavor

Ask these questions before proposing:

1. **Can we source the ingredients reliably?**
   Yes = proceed | No = rethink the flavor

2. **Does it fit our "thick and gooey" brand?**
   Yes = fits brand | No = adapt or drop

3. **Is there a seasonal angle or reason to launch now?**
   Yes = marketing angle | No = find one or wait

4. **Will it hold up in transport for events?**
   Yes = event-safe | No = consider it a retail-only flavor

5. **What's the margin on this vs. the base cost?**
   Good margin = keep | Thin margin = reprice or cut

---

## Submission Process

1. Draft the proposal using this framework
2. Present to Thurman with the full description
3. If approved, generate:
   - Launch copy ( `./scripts/flavor-proposal.js` )
   - Image prompt
   - Social post draft
4. Schedule on calendar for launch day
5. Follow up with mid-run and end-of-run posts
