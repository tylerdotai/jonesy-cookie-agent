# Seasonal Calendar Reference

## Full Seasonal Strategy for Jonesy's Menu

---

## Spring (March–May)

**Theme:** Fresh starts, renewal, outdoor events

**Flavor Ideas:**
- Strawberry Cheesecake (March–April)
- Lemon Blueberry (April–May)
- Easter Egg Sugar Cookies (limited, April)
- Carrot Cake (March–May)

**Campaign Angles:**
- "Perfect for spring weddings"
- "Easter cookie boxes — order by [DATE]"
- "Graduation cookie cups — the gift that actually gets eaten"

**Best posting months:** March, April, May

---

## Summer (June–August)

**Theme:** Berry, refreshment, outdoor events, BBQs

**Flavor Ideas:**
- S'mores (June–August)
- Strawberry Lemonade (June–July)
- Key Lime (June–August)
- Blueberry Cream (July–August)

**Campaign Angles:**
- "Your backyard BBQ deserves better than store-bought"
- "S'mores cookies — campground optional"
- "Beat the Texas heat — ice cream sandwich upgrade available"

**Best posting months:** June, July, August

---

## Back to School (August)

**Theme:** Classroom treats, parent sanity, bulk orders

**Flavor Ideas:**
- Classic Chocolate Chip (always)
- Oatmeal Raisin (nostalgic, wholesome)
- Double Chocolate (for the teachers)

**Campaign Angles:**
- "Teacher appreciation cookies — [DATE] delivery available"
- "Back to school cookie boxes — easy treats for the classroom"
- "Parents: you earned a cookie too"

**Best posting months:** August

**Campaign Angles:**
