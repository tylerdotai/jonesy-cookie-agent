# Content Types Reference

## Overview

Each content type has a specific purpose, platform affinity, and best-use timing. Rotate through them to keep the feed varied and engaging.

---

## 1. Flavor Reveal

**Purpose:** Announce a new or returning flavor.
**Platform:** Instagram, Facebook
**Frequency:** 1-2x/week
**Best timing:** Thursday or Friday (leads into weekend treat orders)

**What to include:**
- Cookie name in first line (hook)
- Sensory description (what it tastes/smells/feels like)
- Price tier (Classic or Premium)
- CTA — "DM to order" or "call (817) 205-1238"
- Urgency element if limited run

**Example caption:**
> I ate so many of these during testing that I had to hide them from myself.
>
> Churro Delight is back. Cinnamon, sugar, and that thick chewy center we've all been dreaming about.
>
> 📍 Corinth, TX | DFW delivering
> 👉 DM to order | (817) 205-1238
>
> #ChurroCookies #DFWCookies #JonesysCookies #GourmetCookies

## 2. Event Promo

**Purpose:** Drive bookings for weddings, corporate events, parties.
**Platform:** Facebook (primary), Instagram (secondary)
**Frequency:** 1x/week
**Best timing:** Monday-Wednesday (people plan events mid-week)

**What to include:**
- Event type in hook
- What Jonesy's provides (mobile setup, fresh-baked day-of)
- Contact info prominently
- Any social proof (past events, clients)

**Example caption:**
> Planning a wedding? Let's talk cookies.
>
> Jonesy's Cookie Company brings the bakery to your reception. Thick, gooey, baked fresh the day of your event.
>
> 📦 Corporate events, weddings, birthday parties, graduations
> 📞 (817) 205-1238
> 📍 Corinth, TX | DFW

## 3. Behind-the-Scenes

**Purpose:** Humanize the brand, show the craft.
**Platform:** Instagram (primary)
**Frequency:** 1x/week
**Best timing:** Sunday or Monday (behind-the-scenes content performs well on weekends)

**What to include:**
- Real process moment (dough, oven, scooping, cooling)
- Short story or context
- Founder voice ("I", "we", not corporate)
- Humble/braggy tone

**Example caption:**
> The secret to thick, gooey cookies? You can't cut corners.
>
> Every batch starts by hand. Every dozen gets tested before it ships.
>
> — Thurman
> Jonesy's Cookie Company | Corinth, TX

---

## 4. Review Spotlight

**Purpose:** Social proof, gratitude, trust building.
**Platform:** Facebook
**Frequency:** As available (when good reviews come in)
**Best timing:** Anytime — post when the review arrives

**What to include:**
- Pull quote from review (1-2 sentences max)
- Brief thank-you from Thurman
- Tag location
- Link to leave a review

**Example caption:**
> "Y'all these are the best cookies I've had in years."
>
> When we read notes like this, it makes the early mornings worth it.
>
> — Thurman
> 🍪 jonesyscookies.com | (817) 205-1238

---

## 5. Weekend Vibe

**Purpose:** Brand building, no specific news or product to announce.
**Platform:** Instagram
**Frequency:** 1x/week
**Best timing:** Friday afternoon (leads into the weekend)

**What to include:**
- Short, punchy, feel-good
- Cookie photo in lifestyle context
- Minimal text — let the image breathe
- Location tag

**Example caption:**
> Friday. You made it.
>
> Go grab some cookies this weekend. Treat yourself. You earned it.
>
> 🍪 Jonesy's Cookie Company — Corinth, TX
> 📞 (817) 205-1238
