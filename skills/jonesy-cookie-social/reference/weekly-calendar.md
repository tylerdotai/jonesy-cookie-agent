## Seasonal Adjustments

### Spring (March-May)
- Add Easter-themed content April
- Push floral/citrus flavors
- "Graduation cookie" season starts May

### Summer (June-August)
- Berry and lemon flavors
- "Perfect for your backyard BBQ" angle
- Back-to-school bulk orders August

### Fall (September-November)
- Pumpkin spice, fall flavors
- "Holiday cookie boxes" pre-sales start November
- Corporate holiday party bookings open

### Holiday (December)
- Gift box promotion
- "Order for your holiday party" angle
- Limited holiday flavors
- "One dozen for Santa" angle

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Instagram (3-4 posts/week)
- Focus: Cookie photography, flavor reveals, behind-the-scenes
- Use Stories for real-time engagement
- Reels optional but recommended for reach

### Facebook (2-3 posts/week)
- Focus: Events, catering, reviews
- Engagement-first: respond to comments quickly
- Use Facebook Live for event setup moments

### Google Business (1-2 posts/month)
- Post seasonal hours, holiday closures
- Share reviews here too

## Scheduling Best Practices

- **Best times to post:** 6-8 PM on weekdays, 10 AM - 1 PM on weekends
- **Consistency > frequency:** 3 great posts beats 7 mediocre ones
- **Batch creation:** Set aside 1 hour/week to draft the full week
- **Approval buffer:** Always get Thurman's approval before scheduling
