## What is this?

An AI agent — think of it like hiring a whole backend team — without the overhead:

- **Social Media Manager** — posts for Instagram + Facebook, generates images, writes captions in YOUR voice, every week
- **Order Manager** — handles inquiry messages, sends quotes, follows up automatically
- **Event Coordinator** — tracks your event calendar, sends day-of logistics to clients, sends prep checklists to you
- **Review Manager** — monitors Google, Yelp, Facebook for new reviews, drafts warm responses for you to approve
- **Flavor Strategist** — proposes seasonal flavors, writes launch copy, plans limited-run campaigns
